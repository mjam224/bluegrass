Indian Fort Mountain

What is cool about this park?

Indian Fort Mountain is located in Berea, Kentucky. It's managed by Beara's College of Forestry, at Indian Fort Mountain lookout you can see all of richmond and parts of EKU and best of all the trail is free to the public
